WHAT
IS CALLED ART?

Art, also called (to distinguish it from craft or design) the fine arts, is a diverse range of human activities that involve the creation of visual, auditory, or performance artifacts that express the creator's intended thoughts to be appreciated for their beauty and emotional power.

7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different art forms are Painting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theater, Film, and Music . However, back in the day, the seven different art forms were called the Liberal Arts, consisting of Grammar, Logic, Rhetoric, Arithmetic, Geometry, Astronomy, and Music.
